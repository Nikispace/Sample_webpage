About

Engineering infrastructure
at high-voltage scale.

With nearly two decades of practice in civil engineering drafting and BIM modeling, Mahalakshmi has built her expertise at the intersection of precision documentation and complex power infrastructure. Her career spans high-voltage GIS substations in Saudi Arabia, 525kV HVDC converter stations for NEOM, and industrial and institutional projects across India.

She brings meticulous command of Revit Architecture, AutoCAD, and MicroStation — from concept-stage schemes through GFC drawings to as-built documentation — with a consistent record of coordinating across civil, structural, mechanical, and electrical disciplines.

From 400MVA transformer foundations to cable tunnel layouts, duct bank systems, and reactor supports, her work underpins the physical infrastructure of Saudi Arabia's power grid and the NEOM megaproject.

  • Substations & converter stations (132kV — 525kV HVDC)
  • Power & utility infrastructure — transformers, reactors, gantries, duct banks
  • Industrial, institutional & commercial buildings
